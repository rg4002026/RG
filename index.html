Honesty first

Is this the
right fit for you?

We'd rather be upfront than waste your time. Premier Outsourcing works exceptionally well for some businesses — and genuinely isn't the right move for others. Here's how to tell the difference.

This is a great fit if you are...
A growing strata firm feeling the pressure of a tight local labour market — good people are hard to find and expensive to keep.
A director or principal spending too much time in the operational weeds — inbox management, levy notices, maintenance follow-ups — instead of building the business.
A business with repeatable admin processes that could be documented, delegated, and handled consistently by a dedicated coordinator.
Ready to invest 2–4 weeks in a proper onboarding — showing someone your systems, your standards, your way of doing things.
Looking to scale your portfolio without an immediate, equivalent increase in local headcount and overheads.
This probably isn't right if you are...
Looking purely for the cheapest option. We're not the lowest-cost provider — we're the most supported one. If price is the only driver, we're not a match.
A solo operator without enough consistent, process-heavy work to keep a full-time coordinator genuinely busy and growing.
Not willing to invest in onboarding. Offshore staffing only works when the client takes the handover seriously. A hire without structure is a hire that underdelivers.
Expecting a set-and-forget solution. Great offshore teams are built, not bought. They need direction, feedback, and a manager who's engaged — even if that's just 15 minutes a day.
Needing someone hired overnight. Our process takes 3–4 weeks. We do it properly — screening, interviewing, onboarding — not just filling a seat fast.

Common questions

Do your offshore staff understand Australian strata? ▾
Honestly — strata-specific knowledge is something we build together with you during onboarding, not something we assume. We hire for strong communication skills, professional attitude, and administrative capability. Your SOPs, your systems, your way of working are then built into how they operate from day one. Think of it like any new hire — except Rhys and the team are alongside you the whole way to make sure they get up to speed fast. Most clients find their coordinator is handling core tasks confidently within 2–3 weeks.
What happens if the hire doesn't work out? ▾
We replace them — free of charge, within 60 days of placement. Beyond 60 days, if performance issues arise, we work with you to manage them directly. Our ongoing KPI oversight means problems rarely go unnoticed. In the rare case a placement hasn't been the right fit, we've always made it right. We're a Melbourne business — our reputation depends on it.
What about time zones — when are they available? ▾
Your offshore coordinator works your hours — Australian business hours, your time zone. This isn't a call centre model. They're a dedicated member of your team, sitting at a desk during your working day, available on your tools (email, Teams, Slack, your PM software — whatever you use). Owners and residents won't know the difference.
My team might push back — how do we handle internal resistance? ▾
This is the most common concern we hear — and it almost always disappears within the first month. The key is framing: this person isn't replacing anyone on your team. They're taking the work nobody wanted — the inbox overflow, the data entry, the maintenance follow-ups. Your local team gets their time back. Once that happens, they become the biggest advocates for the model.
Is $2,900/month really all-inclusive? What's not covered? ▾
The monthly fee covers recruitment, onboarding, HR, payroll, KPI management, and ongoing support. The only thing not included is any software licences you want to give them access to (your strata management system, etc.) — same as any employee. No setup fees, no recruitment surcharges, no surprise invoices.
How is Premier different from a cheaper offshore platform I found online? ▾
The difference is accountability. Offshore freelancer platforms give you a worker and walk away. We're an Australian business, run from Melbourne, with our reputation tied directly to your outcome. We stay involved after placement — ongoing check-ins, KPI reviews, and performance management are part of the service. When something goes wrong (and occasionally it does), you have an Australian team picking up the phone, not a ticket system in another country.
